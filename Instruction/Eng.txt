Adblocker is tested on chrome, edge and firefox. I didn't test Yandex because I was too lazy.

Chrome:
	go to chrome://extensions/ IN THE BROWSER, click Load Unpacked and select the chrome-edge folder.

Edge:
	go to edge://extensions/ IN THE BROWSER, click Load unpacked and select the chrome-edge folder.

Firefox:
	go to about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox IN THE BROWSER, click Load temporary add-on and select the manifest.json file in the firefox folder.
